Kill || Logs

Client app for uploading real-time world PvP statistics for World of Warcraft Classic

Choose the directory where your World of Warcraft Classic logs are located (typically C:\Program Files (x86)\World of Warcraft\_classic_\Logs)
/some/path/log
Before you begin logging, in-game go to SETTTINGS > SYSTEM > NETWORK and ensure you have "Advanced Combat Logging" enabled. Then type /combatlog in your chat box. This begins the recording of data that the Kill Logs client app will read and send to our server when you click the "Start Logging" button below.
